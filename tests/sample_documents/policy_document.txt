Company Policy: Remote Work and Flexible Arrangements
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Policy Number: HR-2026-003

1. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their probation period
(6 months) are eligible for flexible work arrangements. Part-time
employees may apply after 12 months of continuous service.

2. Remote Work Options
2.1 Hybrid Model: Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week.
2.2 Full Remote: Requires director-level approval and is available
only for roles classified as "location-independent" in the HR system.
2.3 Compressed Week: Employees may work 4 x 10-hour days with
manager approval. Friday is the standard day off for compressed
schedules.

3. Equipment and Expenses
The company provides a one-time home office setup allowance of
HKD 5,000 for hybrid employees and HKD 10,000 for full remote
employees. Monthly internet subsidy of HKD 200 is available for
all remote-eligible employees.

4. Performance Requirements
Remote work privileges may be revoked if performance ratings drop
below "Meets Expectations" for two consecutive quarters. Managers
must document specific performance concerns before recommending
revocation.

5. Data Security
Employees working remotely must use company-approved VPN at all
times. Personal devices are not permitted for accessing client
data or internal systems classified as "Confidential" or above.
